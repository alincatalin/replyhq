02. THE LOOP

Don't lose them when they close the app.

Users don't stay in your "Support" view waiting for a reply. We solve the async problem natively.

  • Push Fallback: If the user leaves the app, your chat reply automatically becomes a Push Notification.
  • Slash Snippets: Type /refund or /reset to fire pre-written technical replies instantly.
  • Screenshot Annotation: Users can draw on images before sending.
